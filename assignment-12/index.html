About

Self-taught artist of 25 years, Bryce Rigler has taken 4 years of Graphic Arts
in a Bachelors Program, creating elaborate designs. worlds, and creatures for
various clients. He speciallizes in high-concept creature design and
character design! Born in Livingston,MT on August of 95, he was raised off the
fresh wilderness of Montana throughout his life, developing a
fascination with wildlife as a result, as reflected in his science-fiction
sketches of creatures and animals from other worlds. His interest in character
design stems from his life-long love of animation and has come to appreciate how
characters are designed to reflect their personalities!! Though diagnosed with
Duchennes Muscular Dystrophy at age 4, his lack of brawn doesnt
stop him from creating!
