My Two Favorite Films!!

A Silent Voice

An extraordinary film dealing with self-doubt, bullying,
social anxiety, and coming to terms with your past in a way
that is respectful and emotionally powerful. A film following
the anxieties brought on from a former bully coming to terms with
his mistakes, and moving on to a new future.

Pacific Rim

A bombastic, action-packed passion project by artistic
director Guillermo Del Toro (Hellboy, Pans Labyrinth) with
beautifully directed action sequences and an immense sense of scale
with a simple yet engaging premise of giant monsters rising from the
ocean, and humanity creating mechs to combat them. It understands its
anime inspirations to deliver something original, yet familiar
