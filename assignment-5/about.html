Wayne Douglas Barlowe is a world renouned fantasy
writer as well as creature artist and designer for some
of Hollywood's biggest successes, such as Hellboy, Avatar, and
Pacific Rim! He has written several lavish fantasy/science fiction stories
including his own depiction of Dante's Inferno, Expedition,
(which was turned into a speculative documentary called "Alien Planet"
and his famous "Guide to the Extraterrestrials!"
