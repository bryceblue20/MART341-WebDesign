How to Make a Paper Airplane!!

step by step guide on the most popular form of origami!

  1. fold a 8.5x11 sheet of paper in half vertically
  2. Unfold the paper, and fold both the top corners towards
    the center
  3. Fold the left and right corners towards the center
  4. Fold it in half then unfold
  5. Fold the wings down, matching the top edges up with the
    bottom edge of the body
  6. You now have a paper airplane!
