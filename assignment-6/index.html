Ingredients
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped green onion
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 1/2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper seasoning
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 8 oz baby shrimp and crab meat
  • 2 tbsp sherry wine
  • Chopped parsley and/or cilantro

Directions
  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. melt butter in a large saucepan over
    medium heat; add chopped onion and or celery
    and stir until tender
  3. Blend the flour
  4. Warm the milk in another saucepan over medium
    flame
  5. Add the milk in with the flour/vegetable mixture
    and stir until thickened
  6. add the black pepper,tomato paste, and heavy
    cream
  7. Bring the oven to simmer
  8. Add the shrimp,crab, and sherry wine
  9. Enjoy!
